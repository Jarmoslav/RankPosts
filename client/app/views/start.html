What is RankPosts?

Various internet forums contain hundreds, thousands, even tens of thousands posts within a single thread. A high majority of posts fail to make a meaningful contribution to the discussion in the thread. Nonetheless, there are informed users writing posts that capture important aspects of what is being discussed.

RankPosts presents the most important posts, or parts of posts, and most important users involved in the thread.


Flashback Forum

Analysis and ranking of posts from a number of Flashback Forum threads (https://www.flashback.org/), one of the largest internet forum sites in Sweden.

How?

The ranking presented by RankPosts is entirely based on mathematical algorithms and without involvement of any person.
